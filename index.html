Seville

THE CITY THAT IS FASCINATING THE WORLD

Many define Seville as special. Its culture, traditions and artistic heritage made this city famous worldwide; it's the birthplace of flamenco and the center of the South of Spain, a vibrant city full of life.

Enjoy the sun and the scent of jasmine in its streets, taste the delicious tapas and learn about Seville's history through its innumerable monuments.

Three things you can't miss in Seville

Dive into its historical legacy

The gothic Cathedral, Plaza de España and the Real Alcázar are some of the most important monuments in Seville, but there are picturesque spots in every corner

Discover the local gastronomy

Try delicious dishes like gazpacho, cod with tomato, spinach with chickpeas, serranito or torrijas, served in small dishes called tapas. Yummy!

Flamenco and Spanish guitar shows

One of the best experiences in Seville are flamenco shows. Amazing dancers accompanied by live musicians and singers, don't miss it!
